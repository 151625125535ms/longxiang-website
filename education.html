Education & School-Enterprise Cooperation

Longxiang connects industry resources, engineering practice, and research collaboration to cultivate high-quality electrical and new energy talent.

Co-built Industrial College

In 2016, Longxiang and Henan Mechanical and Electrical Vocational College co-built Longxiang Electrical Engineering College as a model platform for deep industry-education integration.

The cooperation focuses on power system automation, electrical automation, power supply technology, distributed generation, and smart microgrid education.

Professional programs are connected with real industry chains, practical engineering projects, and Longxiang's electrical equipment manufacturing base.

Training Scenes

From classroom discussion to field operation, Longxiang turns engineering practice into visible learning scenarios.

Classroom & Project Discussion

Coursework combines technical lectures, project review, and engineering case discussion so students understand both equipment principles and application scenarios.

  • Technical lectures and practical case reviews
  • Project-based teaching connected to industrial parks
  • Dual-mentor guidance from teachers and engineers

Field Practical Training

Students train in outdoor electrical scenarios with safety supervision, hands-on tasks, and real operation procedures.

  • Line-field teaching and alternating study-training model
  • Power equipment, photovoltaic, wind power, and energy storage scenarios
  • On-post internships that prepare students for engineering work

Equipment Platform Practice

Training platforms help students understand electrical system structure, operation logic, testing, maintenance, and project delivery workflow.

  • Electrical control and switchgear platforms
  • Hands-on skills assessment and teaching-production training
  • Practical hardware rooted in Longxiang manufacturing experience
