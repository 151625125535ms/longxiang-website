عن Longxiang Electrical

شركة تقنية عالية متخصصة في معدات نقل وتوزيع الطاقة.

Henan Longxiang Electrical Co., Ltd.

Founded in 2003 and listed on the NEEQ, Longxiang Electrical provides intelligent, low-carbon power equipment for global power distribution projects.

With modern manufacturing bases, long-term research partnerships, and a broad transformer product portfolio, Longxiang Electrical supports utilities, industrial projects, renewable energy systems, and international engineering partners.

Our product portfolio covers oil-immersed transformers, dry-type transformers, amorphous alloy transformers, switchgear, reactive power compensation equipment, and project-specific distribution equipment.
